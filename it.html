New York is huge and so are some of the Big Tech companies out there. These IT places listed below are all located in Manhattan.Some are Even a minute away from Times Square. Some of the Top places to work are Adobe, Ebay, and Apple.

Adobe

Adobe - New York is in a minute walk from Times Square. Adobe is most well known for the multimedia and creativity software products. This company cares not just about its products but also its employees it’s been on Fortune Magazine's list of Best Workplaces.

Ebay

Ebay is one of the largest e-commerce corporation. From what I've read online on different job review sites People who work here. Honestly enjoy the Work life balance and culture.They are actively helping grow the City’s tech community.

Apple fifth Avenue

Reopened just recently, Apple's Fifth Avenue is a Beautiful Apple store that is in the heart of manhattan. Apple has always been a well known IT Giant in the tech world. This Location has a fun environment, enriching customers, and most importantly, working here you get major discounts.
